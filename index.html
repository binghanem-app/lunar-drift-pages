Lunar Drift

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

Lunar Drift is a single-player arcade game for iOS. We built it so that it needs none of your personal information.

The short version

Lunar Drift does not collect, store, or share any personal data. It works entirely offline. There are no accounts, no ads, no analytics, and no third-party tracking.

Information we collect

None. The app does not ask for, collect, or transmit any personal information — no name, email, location, contacts, photos, or device identifiers.

Data stored on your device

The app saves two small settings locally on your device, using Apple's standard on-device storage:

  • Your best score.
  • Your sound on/off preference.

This information never leaves your device, is not accessible to us, and is removed if you delete the app.

Network use

Lunar Drift does not connect to the internet. It sends and receives no data.

Third parties

The app contains no third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking software. No data is shared with anyone.

Children's privacy

Lunar Drift is suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

If we add features in the future that change how data is handled (for example, online leaderboards), we will update this policy and revise the date above.
