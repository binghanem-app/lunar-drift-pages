Common questions

How do I play?
Hold anywhere on the screen to fire your engine and rise. Release to let gravity pull you back down. Dodge the asteroids, collect stars and shields, and see how far you can get.

My best score didn't save / reset.
Best score is stored only on your device. Reinstalling the app or restoring a device as new will reset it — this can't be recovered, since Lunar Drift keeps no data on any server.

Does the app require an internet connection?
No. Lunar Drift works fully offline.
